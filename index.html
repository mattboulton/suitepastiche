Suite Pastiche

A third space to gather, listen, and dance.

Fusing Oldenburg's third place and Mancuso's Loft principles, Suite Pastiche is a rendezvous for social intercourse, sonic bathing and somatic expression.

We share high-fidelity, borderless sound through vintage hardware, based on an organic, invite-a-friend approach.

If you'd like to gather, listen and dance at one of our events, contact us.

"Real listening can be neither improved nor perfected, for it is perfection itself."
